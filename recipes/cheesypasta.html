Cheesy Pasta Recipe

Description

This dish is really simple to make because the oven does all of the heavy lifting

While your oven is preheating to 200 degrees celsius, take your oven proof dish and place your block of Boursin soft cheese in the middle. Pour your cherry tomatoes around the cheese. Add some mixed herbs and that is now ready to go. Next, measure out as much pasta as you would like. This needs to be cooked during the last 10 minutes of the overall cooking time to ensure everything is ready at the same time. Put the oven proof dish in the oven for 20 minutes. Once everything has been cooked, drain your pasta, mix the cheesy, tomato'y dish so you have a cheesy sauce. Stir your pasta in and dish up!

Ingredients

  • Boursin soft cheese
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • 75g pasta per person

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 200 degrees celsius
  2. Measure your pasta, enough per person
  3. Place block of boursin in the centre of an oven proof dish
  4. Pour tomaotoes around the block of cheese in the oven proof dish, adding mixed herbs
  5. Cook cheese and tomatoes for 20 minutes
  6. Cook pasta for 10 minutes
  7. Remove cheese and tomato mix from the oven and stir so ingredients combine
  8. Drain pasta
  9. Stir pasta into the cheese and tomato mix
  10. Serve
