Curry

Description

This is a simple curry using ingredients you can find at most UK supermarkets

Whilst not included in the recipe we recommend you pick up a pack of poppadoms or naan bread as a side.

Ingredients

  • Patacks Curry Pot
  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 125g chicken breast per person
  • 75g of basmati rice per person
  • Half a diced onion

Steps

  1. Fry the diced onion in a pan with some oil / fry light for 1-2 minutes
  2. Add the chopped chicken breast and cook until there is no more pink
  3. Once you are happy with the chicken add the tin of chopped tomaotoes. Stir.
  4. Add the pot of curry and and stir until it is spread through the existing sauce. Let this simmer on a lowe heat.
  5. Boil water and cook the rice. This takes roughly 10 minutes.
  6. Dran your rice, seperate into bowls
  7. Add the curry
  8. Serve with sides
