Sausage Pasta

Description

This is a quick and easy pasta dish that is ready in less than 15 minutes cooking time.

Using stir in sauce cuts down on time after those long days at work. A favourite for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Sausages (2-3 per person)
  • 75g of pasta per person
  • Dolmio stir in pasta sauce pot

Steps

  1. Grill the sausages whole (following advice on packaging)
  2. Boil and cook the pasta
  3. Once cooked drain the pasta and add it back to the pan
  4. Add and mix the stir in sauce
  5. Once grilled, chop the sausages and add them to the saucy pasta
  6. Share between bowls and serve
